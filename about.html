My name is Viktoria Schneider, I was born in the last century and am based in Vienna but live anywhere where my music is.

What music I like? Whatever moves me. Emotionally as well as physically.
To be precise: Metalcore, Harder Styles, Psytrance. And mixes of those.
To be even more precise: all Metalcore subgenres, Hardstyle, Rawstyle, and the best: Electronicore. To get an idea of what I love most, I recommend you listen to The Browning and Riot Shift.

I love music, audiovisual stuff, music events, videography, and dancing. And lasershows. So you'll most likely find me dancing arount at festivals and raves, taking videos there or creating trippy dance videos.
You can hire me to do that for you! I'll dance / film / live there for you. I can also be your sound guy.
